Welcome to the ACM

Create automatic, on-chain, recurring alarm clocks to execute arbitrary smart contract functions. There are several prerequisites for a functional Alarm Clock:

  1. A delegated wallet funded with ether
  2. A payment delegate assigned as a delegate of the wallet
  3. A gas price oracle to predict the cost of alarm execution at a future date

An alarm clock can be destroyed, cancelled, or rescheduled at any time by a wallet delegate.

Provided Delegates

  • Decentralized Payment Delegate (DPD). The DPD allows any delegate to schedule a payment with their respective wallet. The DPD must also be a delegate of the wallet.
  • Recurring Alarm Clock Wizard (RACW). The RACW allows any delegate of a wallet to create a recurring alarm clock and schedule it with a provided payment delegate. The RACW must also be a delegate of the wallet if the payment delegate provided is the DPD.
