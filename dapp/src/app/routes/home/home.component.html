Recurring Transactions on the Ethereum Blockchain

  1. Sign in to your Ethereum Account
  2. Deploy your Everchain Wallet
  3. Create your first Recurring Transaction (RTx)

Transaction Costs

RTx Cost = RTx Gas Used * Current Gas Price * Safety Multiplier

The current gas price is set by the Bancor network. The Bancor Network needs a maximum gas price to prevent front running but an RTx repurposes this value as the best current gas price.

The Alarm Cost Safety Multiplier is a temporary measure to ensure in the event of a drastic price drop or spike in gas costs, the alarm will still have enough funds to execute. In the future, a new mechanism can be created that funds alarms at the time of execution and removes the need for an oracle. In addition, the new mechanism will also enable paying in any desired ERC20 token such as Dai.
